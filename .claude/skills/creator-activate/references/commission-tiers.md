# Commission Tiers & Deal Reference

## Standard Commission Tiers

All MFL affiliate contracts use the same tiered commission structure:

| New Paying Referrals (Monthly) | Commission Rate |
|-------------------------------|-----------------|
| 0 users | 5% |
| 1–2 users | 10% |
| 3–5 users | 12% |
| 6–20 users | 15% |
| 21+ users | 20% |

**Monthly calculation**: The tier is re-evaluated each month based on the number of new paying referrals brought in that month (not lifetime). The rate applies to that month's referee primary sales volume.

**Payout**: `max(tier % × monthly referee primary sales volume, minimum guarantee)`. The guarantee is a floor, not additive — the creator receives whichever is higher.

## Net Revenue Definition

Net Revenue = Primary Market purchases by referred users, minus:
- Chargebacks
- Secondary sales
- Credits
- Fees
- Tax

**Attribution window:** Revenue tracked for up to 1 year per referred user from their first purchase. Sales from a referred user within that window count toward the monthly referee primary sales volume even if the referral was brought in a prior month.

## Deal Types

- **Affiliate Only**: Commission-only, no guarantee
- **Affiliate + Guarantee**: Monthly minimum guarantee + commission (most common)
- **Flat Fee**: Fixed monthly payment, no commission (rare)
