## Messaging Rules

Follow `creators-management/messaging-style.md` for tone:
- No em dashes. Use periods instead.
- Line breaks between paragraphs
- Casual, friendly tone
- "Looking forward to it!" as closer (not "I'm looking forward to it!")
- Save to `/tmp/msg-<slug>-kickoff.md` AND print in terminal
