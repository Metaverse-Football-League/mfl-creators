# Content Audit

**Expected weekly content:** YT: 2 livestreams/videos + 1 sponsored livestream weekly | X: 4 tweets weekly

## Observations

- Andrew launched the **dedicated MFL YouTube channel** (`@MFLwithLaird`) in early April 2025 alongside his contract start. He also cross-posted 2 MFL intro videos on his main Sorare channel (`@SorarewithLaird`, ~5K subs) which got significantly more views (811 vs 233 for "What is MFL?").
- **Two distinct phases** in YouTube output:
  - **Apr–Jul 2025 (W14–W28):** High-energy launch period. 35 videos/streams in 15 weeks (~2.3/week). Peak was W23 with 6 streams in a single week. Strong engagement, views often 500-1800. Multiple CEO interviews, community collaborations.
  - **Aug–Dec 2025 (W29–W52):** Steady decline to ~1 video/week. 24 weeks produced only 22 videos. Views dropped to 200-500 range. More solo content, fewer guest streams. 5 empty weeks including holiday break.
- **2026 (W01–W10):** Further slowdown. 7 YouTube uploads in 10 weeks (0.7/week), all livestreams. Engagement remains solid when he does publish (268-409 views, high like ratios). Guest collaborations continue (Ryan Walter in W09, Hamez in W10).
- **Content is almost entirely livestreams** from W19 onward. Pre-recorded edited videos are rare — mostly short clips (1-5 min) alongside long-form streams (1-2 hrs).
- **YouTube deliverables consistently below contract:** Contract calls for 3 videos/week (2 regular + 1 sponsored). Actual average across 48 weeks: ~1.4/week. Only 3 weeks ever hit or exceeded 3 videos. No identifiable sponsored livestreams at any point.
- **X activity** (audited for 2026 only): consistent but generally below the 4-post/week target. Most weeks 2-6 MFL posts. W05 had zero X activity.
- **No Twitch activity** across the entire audit period.
- **Guest collaborations** are a strength: Trust the Process (recurring), NepentheZ, JoshXLs, Ryan Walter, Jimbo, CEO Mathurin Blouin (2 interviews). These generate higher engagement.
