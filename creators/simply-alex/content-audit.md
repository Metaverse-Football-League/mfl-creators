# Content Audit

**Expected weekly content:** YT: 3 dedicated/mo + 3 integrations/mo + 3 Shorts/mo | X: 12 tweets/mo (~3/week)

## Observations

SimplyAlex has been a consistent and enthusiastic MFL content creator since mid-January 2026. The "Haaland's Race to Div 1" series ran 9 episodes in Season 1 (EP1–EP9) plus a standalone Beginner Guide, and Season 2 launched in W10 with Episode 1. Although some episode titles lack the "MFL" keyword, transcript analysis confirms the entire series is 100% MFL gameplay — all episodes are classified as Dedicated. With transcript verification, several videos with only an affiliate link in the description (without actual MFL discussion in the video) are classified as description-only. True integrations — where MFL is verbally mentioned and discussed within Sorare-focused videos — average 800–1,600 views, providing broader audience exposure. Dedicated MFL videos average ~170 views but show genuine engagement (high like ratios). On X, SimplyAlex is organically engaged with the MFL community — sharing game moments, player progressions, match reactions, and promoting episodes. X posts average 500–2,000 impressions with a peak of 2,168 on "Anyone got decent value cards." Against contract deliverables (3 dedicated + 3 integrations + 3 Shorts per month on YT, 12 tweets/mo on X), YouTube dedicated output dipped in W09–W10 with only 1 dedicated video across two weeks (Season 2 EP1 in W10), though desc-only Sorare videos continue to carry the MFL affiliate link. X data unavailable for W09–W10 (API token not set). No Shorts detected across the entire audit period.
