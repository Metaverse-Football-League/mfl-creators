# Content Audit

**Expected weekly content:** YT: 1 dedicated + 3 sponsored videos + Friday livestream | X: 3 tweets

## Observations

- Quinny produces a high volume of content overall (2-3 regular videos + 3-7 livestreams per week), but the vast majority is Sorare-focused. MFL appears consistently in video descriptions (referral link) but rarely in spoken video content outside dedicated MFL videos.
- Dedicated MFL YouTube videos cluster around season start/end events (Season 10 review, Season 11 prep). During the 11-week audit period, only 2 dedicated MFL videos were uploaded (both in W01), with zero dedicated MFL videos from W02 through W11. This is well below the expected 1 dedicated video per week.
- MFL-titled livestreams remain strong -- Quinny streams dedicated MFL sessions for squad building, tactical setup, matchday, and season kickoffs. W08-W11 shows a pickup with Season 12 content (tactical setup, kickoff, club staff, Monday Night Football). These are substantial streams (30-100+ minutes) and represent genuine MFL engagement.
- The "3 MFL-sponsored YouTube videos" deliverable is ambiguous. All Sorare videos include the MFL referral link in the description, but none of the Sorare-titled videos mention MFL in the actual video content (confirmed via transcript analysis across all weeks). These are classified as Desc-Only.
- X/Twitter activity remains very low. W10 and W11 had zero MFL posts found. The expected 3 tweets/week is rarely met -- only W01 came close.
- Quinny is clearly a genuine MFL player and ambassador (active in Discord, runs multiple clubs, deeply engaged with game mechanics), but the contractual deliverables for dedicated MFL content on YouTube and X are consistently not being met.
