### Product Ideas Contributed
- **Ranked Quick Play mode**: Always-available ranked matchmaking aligned with the 6-week season cycle. Players gain/lose skill points based on friendly match results, can use any of their teams, and earn minor rewards. ELO gain/loss should account for team strength differential. Addresses the "nothing to do" problem for eliminated cup teams or mid-table clubs. Appeals to the "gamer" audience who want active tactical experimentation without fitness/season constraints. Could also serve as a testing ground for Live Changes (in-match tactical adjustments, already developed on staging). Inspired by Football Manager Online (Korea) and WoW Arenas
- **Alliance-based competitions**: Regular alliance tournaments with leaderboards, building on the Goal Rush format
- **Quests/daily challenges**: Play matches in specific formations for bonus skill points, daily login incentives
- **Cosmetic rewards**: Pitch patterns or kit patterns for top-ranked managers each season
- **Rapid Play expansion**: Always-available rapid play with entry fees (as an MFL sink) rather than free reward-giving tournaments
- **World Cup tournament with non-NFT national teams**: Free-to-enter tournament where participants manage pre-generated 45-player national squads representing their country. Lets new users experience the tactical depth and match engine on equal footing without buying a club. Cosmetic rewards for participants (e.g., national team kit patterns). Serves as both onboarding and a re-engagement mechanism for existing users
- **AI-generated season retrospectives**: Use image generation to create visual recaps in HQ — cup final results, top scorers, most-used players, consecutive win streaks, historical team lineups. Enables users to browse and relive past seasons. Addresses the gap in capturing club history and past achievements within the game
