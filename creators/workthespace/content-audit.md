## Observations

WorkTheSpace's contract began 2026-W07 (Feb 11, 2026). No MFL content was detected in the pre-contract period (W01-W06). In the first contract week (W07), there was a single X reply mentioning MFL but no YouTube content. In W08, two FM26 livestreams included "MFL Later?" in the title — transcript analysis showed Stream 1 had only a ~10-second MFL mention while Stream 2 contained ~40 minutes of dedicated MFL gameplay from 1:50:29 onward with a custom MFL overlay. Since W08, there has been a three-week gap (W09-W11) with zero MFL content detected across any platform — no YouTube MFL segments, no X tweets, no Twitch MFL content. During this period, WorkTheSpace continued his regular FM26 content (daily Park to Prem episodes + 2-3 Hexagon Challenge livestreams per week) but none included MFL mentions in titles or descriptions. This means across 5 contract weeks (W07-W11), only 1 X post and 2 YouTube integrations have been produced — well below the expected ~1 weekly livestream segment and ~4 monthly tweets. No dedicated standalone MFL video has been produced. The lack of MFL branding in stream titles/descriptions since W08 is notable given the contract requirement. A YouTube /videos timeout warning was raised during W09-W11 data collection, though manual verification via playlist-items confirmed no missed MFL content.
