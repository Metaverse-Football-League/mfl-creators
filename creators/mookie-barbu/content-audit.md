## Observations

Mookie's contract started Jan 19, 2026. His launch post on Jan 21 was exceptional — 97K impressions, 111 likes, 24 RTs — demonstrating strong reach with his 109K-follower audience. He followed up with a second original post about red cards (7K impressions) and actively replied to community comments during W04. After Jan 24 he went completely silent on MFL for over a month (W05–W08 — zero content). He briefly returned in W09 (Feb 25–26) with a season recap post (4.7K impressions, 67 likes) and self-RTs, but has since gone dark again — W10 and W11 show zero MFL content, bringing his total inactive weeks to 6 out of 8 contract weeks.

Key concerns:
- **Consistency**: Only 2 active weeks out of 8 contract weeks (W04–W11). Six weeks with zero MFL content (W05–W08, W10–W11).
- **Deliverables gap**: The pinned thread and giveaway deliverables have never been observed across 8 contract weeks.
- **Post volume**: Even in active weeks, original posts (2 in W04, 1 in W09) fall far short of the 3 tweets/week target. Total: 3 original posts in 8 weeks vs. 24 expected.
- **Engagement quality**: When he does post, engagement is strong — his audience responds well to MFL content. But frequency is the critical issue.
- **Pattern**: The W09 comeback was a one-off, not a sustained return. The creator appears disengaged from deliverables.
