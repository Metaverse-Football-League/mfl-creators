### Product Ideas Contributed
- **Pack ballot system**: Instead of fastest-finger-first allocation, implement a registration window where interested users sign up, then packs are randomly allocated via lottery. Solves frustration around internet speed / luck determining who gets packs
- **Loan revenue share limits**: Suggested setting caps on loan revenue share percentages in competitive divisions — teams with 96% revenue share from loans competing in Silver division undermines owners who invest
- **Retired player roles**: Retired players could have a continued use within the game as coaches, scouts, or club legends — gives long-term utility to aging assets
- **Academy / youth system**: More young players coming through the game, allowing long-term development over 10+ seasons

### Feedback Given
- Engine inconsistency between seasons is the primary frustration — tactics that work one season become ineffective the next

- Overall team standard in Iron league is very high — competitive even with 80-83 rated players; needs player retirements to rebalance supply
- MFL's simplicity (6 stats, pick-up-and-play) is an advantage compared to Sorare's increasing complexity
